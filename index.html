Biography
Yongyuan (Cheryl) Liang is a researcher focused on developing robust, versatile, and efficient intelligent agents for AI systems. She actively explores both theoretical frameworks and empirical findings, with specific research interests in Reinforcement Learning, Foundation Models, and Trustworthy AI. She joins UMD CS as a PhD student advised by Furong Huang and works closely with Huazhe Xu.
